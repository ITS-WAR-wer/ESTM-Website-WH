Agriculture Class

Votech-3


Class 3-6 and 4-6


Class 3-6 and 4-6 are classes in which general knowledge of Science along with Agriculture are instructed to students. The main objective of this course is to instruct students about the importance of the science field to the agricultural field and vice-versa.
Student practicals for this class include:
  • Crop Harvesting
  • Raising of Livestock
  • Chemistry Labs
  • Cooking Practicals

4 semester course Subjects :
  • Mathematics (Major)
  • English (Major)
  • Information and Communication Technology (Major)
  • Family and Resource Management(Major)
  • Agriculture (Major)
  • Human and Social Biology (Major)
  • Chemistry(Supplementary)
  • Social Studies (minor)
  • Spanish (Minor)
