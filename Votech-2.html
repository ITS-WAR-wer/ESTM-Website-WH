Class 3-5 and 4-5


Class 3-5 and 4-5 are classes in which general knowledge of basic Technical Drawing, wood-work, and mechanical engineering are instructed to students. The main objective of this course is to instruct students about the importance of vocational sector to Belize.
Student practicals for this class include:
  • Welding Practicals
  • B&FT Practicals
  • TD Drawings and Scaled Model of Infrastructure

4 semester course Subjects :
  • Mathematics (Major)
  • English (Major)
  • Information and Communication Technology (Major)
  • Technical Drawing (Major)
  • Mechanical Engineering (Major)
  • Building and Furniture (Major)
  • Human and Social Biology(Supplementary)
  • Social Studies (minor)
  • Spanish (Minor)
