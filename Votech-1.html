Electrical Class

Votech-1


Class 3-4 and 4-4


Class 3-4 and 4-4 are classes in which general knowledge of electricity, wood-work, and mechanical engineering are instructed to students. The main objective of this course is to instruct students about the importance of vocational sector to Belize..
Student practicals for this class include:
  • Welding Practicals
  • B&FT Practicals
  • Wiring Practicals
  • TD Drawings and Scaled Model of Infrastructure

4 semester course Subjects :
  • Mathematics (Major)
  • English (Major)
  • Information and Communication Technology (Major)
  • Electricity (Major)
  • Technical Drawing (Major)
  • Mechanical Engineering (Eligible)
  • Building and Furniture (Eligible)
  • Physics(Supplementary)
  • Chemistry (Supplementary)
  • Social Studies (minor)
  • Spanish (Minor)
