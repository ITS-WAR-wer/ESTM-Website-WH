ESTM Student Handbook

Escuela Secundaria Técnica México provides a comprehensive student handbook that outlines the rules and regulations of the school. The handbook is designed to guide students on the expectations and responsibilities of being a student at ESTM. It covers various aspects of school life, including academic policies, disciplinary procedures, and extracurricular activities.

Students are encouraged to familiarize themselves with the contents of the handbook and abide by the rules and regulations outlined within. The handbook serves as a valuable resource for students to refer to when they have questions or concerns about school policies and procedures.

Parents and guardians are also encouraged to review the student handbook to gain an understanding of the expectations and requirements of their child's education at ESTM. By working together with the school, parents can help ensure that their child has a positive and successful learning experience.
